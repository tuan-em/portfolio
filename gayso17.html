Gãy Số 17

Year: 2023. Type: Rave Event, Visual Jockey.
Client: Nhạc Gãy. Role: Visual Artist, VJ.


Gãy Số 17 is the 17th rave event hosted by Nhac Gay in Saigon. With a desire to connect & explore a shared identity, it is an interaction of club culture, contemporary art, live performance, & video. Những Cái Góc is pleased to be the VJ for the Mặt Đất section, which is more serene with ambient music & performing arts. There's also room for us to do projection mapping with the environment of the club.

Tuan or Tuan Em, is a Saigon-based junior digital designer driven by his love for visual arts and motion graphics. With diverse experiences in 2D and 3D graphics, graphic design, VFX, UX/UI, screen printing, and VJ/DJ, they all shaped his own journey in art and design. His creative work embodies his background, artistic dedication, and the insightful perspective of a young Viet identity navigating the world. Always eager to experiment, collaborate, and embrace new learning experiences, he aims to create thought-provoking pieces. Tuan is also an angle of @nhungcaigoc collectives.

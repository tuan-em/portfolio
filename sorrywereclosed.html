Sorry, we|RE CLOSED

Year: 2024. Type: Photography Series
Role: All Role.


Sorry, we|re CLOSED is a photography series capturing the quiet, isolated spaces around Melbourne at night. With this project, I want viewers to have a closer look at the hidden beauty and melancholy in these quiet moments. Not only illustrating the loneliness of closed spaces, the project is also a tribute to the workers who keep shops alive during the day. It is a form of gratitude toward everyday life.

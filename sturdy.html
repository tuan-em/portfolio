StuRDY

Year: 2023. Type: Learning Management System Design.
Role: UX/UI Designer.


StuRDY is an LMS designed for students, especially part-time university students having a hard time navigating between work and study. With a Pomodoro timer, progress bar, blended schedules, e-learning, and other features, StuRDY goes beyond simply delivering content. We create a learning ecosystem by integrating into their lives and supporting them academically & personally, making the experience a fufilling journey.

GEELY x Chi Pu

Year: 2025. Type: Launching Event, Visual LED.
Client: Geely, Microwave Soup. Role: Visual Artist.


GEELY x Chi Pu is a live performance for Geely Auto's brand launch event in Vietnam, officially marking its first entry into the Vietnamese market. It is a 20-minute performance by Chi Pu with 5 phases to represent the "multi-energy" spirits of the brand. As a member of Những Cái Góc, I had an opportunity to work with the visual team under the leadership of Microwave Soup as a 3D Motion Designer of Phase 2.

Trần Thế (2024)

Year: 2024. Type: Album Trailer, VFX. Client: The Tripod Guys.
Role: VFX Artist, VFX Breakdown Video Editor.


Trần Thế is a debut album by Thể Thiên. The trailer for this album project produced by The Tripod Guys will take you on a journey of Seven Deadly Sins. Working with Những Cái Góc and Wannasaur on the post-production, I was in charge some VFX elements and let frances Pham composite them with on-set footages. I also edited the VFX Breakdown Videos with the help of Chú Chuột Dừa and my collective.

Vinauthorised

Year: 2024. Type: Audio-Visual, Visual Jockey.
Role: Visual Artist.


Vinauthorised is a 30-minute Audio-Visual set made by Nhung Cai Goc & PH00000NG for an event called VinA/V, organised by RMIT SCD Vietnam & V2X Academy. We as a team collaborated with PH000000NG telling unauthorised events, actions, incidents in Vietnam. Inspired by the Thoi Su 19h, we arranged the set to have a similar format to a news program on TV. I got to develop two micro themes within this macro theme.

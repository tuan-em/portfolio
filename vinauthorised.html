Vinauthorised

Year: 2023. Type: Audio-Visual, Visual Jockey.
Role: Visual Artist.


Vinauthorised is a 30-minute Audio-Visual set made by Những Cái Góc & PH00000NG for an event called VinA/V, organised by RMIT SCD Vietnam & V2X Academy. We as a team collaborated with PH000000NG telling unauthorised events, actions, incidents in Vietnam. Inspired by the Thoi Su 19h, we arranged the set to have a similar format to a news program on TV. I got to develop two micro themes within this macro theme.

Cho Dai Bay (Dogs Peeing Bay)

Peeing in public has been an issue in Vietnam for decades. Even if the roads are full of vehicles, it’s not really hard to find a person peeing in public places. It is an embarrassing action but has engraved in every perrson, so it feels normal. The visual of a person facing their back to the camera while they are facing the wall attracts me to create visual loops. I want to convey with the message, “Peeing in public could happen at any place, and it could just be anyone”. Moreover, these people are visualised as dogs. They raise their legs, wag their tails, and pee like a dog because "Đái Bậy Là Chó".




Lan Dot Bien (Mutant Orchids)

Mutant orchids became a gold rush in 2021, starting in July 2020 with news of a Juliet orchid being negotiated for 83 million VND. This sparked a wave of viral stories about orchids selling for billions, attracting hopeful investors. However, many of these deals were part of a get-rich-quick scam targeting rural communities. After scammers cashed in, prices crashed in 2022 which make the mutant orchids became cheaper than water spinach. These actions could be prosecuted under Vietnam’s 2015 Criminal Code for fraud. The media often refers to this as “leading people by the nose” which inspired me to visualise the victims as cows.
